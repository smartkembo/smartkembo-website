FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use this?
No. We handle all the technical setup — Mikrotik configuration, portal setup, and payment integration. You just manage your business from the dashboard.
How fast does internet start after payment?
Between 5 and 30 seconds after the customer confirms M-Pesa payment, their device is automatically allowed through the router.
Where does the money go?
Directly to your M-Pesa business number. SmartKembo never holds or passes through your customer payments. We only charge you the monthly subscription fee.
What happens if a customer's time expires?
The system automatically disconnects them from the internet. They need to purchase a new package to reconnect. No manual action needed from you.
Can I change my package prices?
Yes, anytime. Log into your dashboard, go to Packages, and update prices or add new ones. Changes take effect immediately on the customer portal.
What if I want to stop using the service?
Simply stop paying the monthly fee. Your account will be suspended automatically. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no paperwork required.
