How It Works

01
📱
Customer Pays
Customer sends M-Pesa to your business number. They choose litres — 10L, 20L or 50L.
02
⚡
System Confirms
SmartKembo backend receives M-Pesa webhook instantly and confirms the payment.
03
🚰
Valve Opens
IoT dispenser valve opens automatically. Releases exact litres paid — then closes.
04
💰
Money is Yours
Revenue goes directly to your M-Pesa. View all sales on your dashboard in real time.
