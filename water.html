How SWV Works

Simple for the customer — powerful for the owner.

01
🪙
Customer Inserts Coins
They insert TZS coins — one or more. The LCD displays their balance and how many litres they will receive at the current price.
02
▶
Press One Button
A single button press. The Arduino Mega opens the solenoid valve and water starts flowing immediately.
03
💧
Exact Litres Dispensed
The YF-S201 flow sensor counts litres with ±0.1L accuracy. The valve closes automatically — their litres only, not a drop more.
04
📊
You See It in Real Time
The ESP32 sends transaction data to the server within minutes. View revenue and litres on your dashboard — live, from your phone.
